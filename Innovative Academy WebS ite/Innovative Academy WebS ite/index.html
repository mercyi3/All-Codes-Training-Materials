Freequently Asked Question

Welcome to the FAQ section of Innovative Academy. Here, you’ll find answers to some of the most commonly asked questions about our courses, training programs, and placement assistance. Should you have any additional inquiries, please do not hesitate to contact us.

What courses does Innovative Academy offer?

At Innovative Academy, we offer a wide range of courses in Networking, Cloud Computing, and Software Technologies. Some of our popular courses include:
  • CCNA (Cisco Certified Network Associate)
  • AWS (Amazon Web Services)
  • DevOps
  • Python and Java Programming
  • Software Development
  • Cloud Computing (Azure, Google Cloud)
  • CCNP (Cisco Certified Network Professional)
Our programs are structured to accommodate individuals at all skill levels, from novices to seasoned professionals. We begin with foundational concepts and progressively advance to more complex subjects

What makes Innovative Academy different from other institutes?

Innovative Academy stands out due to:
  • Industry-Aligned Curriculum: Our courses are designed to keep pace with industry trends and technologies.
  • Live Projects & Hands-On Training: We focus on practical learning with live projects and real-world scenarios
  • Experienced Trainers: Learn from certified trainers who have years of experience in their respective fields.
  • 100% Placement Assistance: We help students build their resumes, conduct mock interviews, and provide job referrals to ensure placement success.
  • Flexible Learning Options: We offer weekday, weekend, and online classes to fit various schedules.

How do I enroll in a course at Innovative Academy?

Enrolling at Innovative Academy is simple! You can:
  • Visit our website and navigate to the course section.
  • Choose your desired program.
  • Complete the registration form or reach out to our admissions team for assistance.
  • Our team will assist you with the course details, schedule, and payment options.
Alternatively, you can also call us or email to get more information.

Is there a placement guarantee?

While we cannot guarantee job placement, Innovative Academy offers 100% placement assistance. We provide resume building, mock interview sessions, and job referrals to help you secure employment. Our committed placement cell collaborates closely with leading IT firms to facilitate connections between you and prospective employers.. Over 10,000 students have successfully been placed through our placement assistance program.

What is the duration of the courses?

The duration of our courses varies based on the program and mode of training (classroom, online, weekend). On average:
  • Short-term courses like CCNA and AWS: 1.5 to 2 months.
  • Advanced courses like DevOps and Cloud Computing: 3 to 4 months.
For a more accurate estimate, please reach out to our admissions team or refer to the specific course details on our website.

Are the courses suitable for beginners?

Yes! Our programs are structured to accommodate individuals at all skill levels, from novices to seasoned professionals. We begin with foundational concepts and progressively advance to more complex subjects. Whether you have prior knowledge or not, our trainers ensure that you understand the core concepts and gain hands-on experience through practical training.

Do you offer online training?

Yes, we offer online training for most of our courses. Our online programs are designed to provide the same high-quality learning experience as classroom sessions. You have the opportunity to participate in live, interactive sessions and to view recorded lectures at your convenience.

What is the fee structure for courses?

The fee structure is contingent upon the specific course selected. We offer flexible payment options, and discounts may be available for group enrollments. For detailed fee information, please contact our admissions team or visit the specific course page on our website.

How do I prepare for certification exams after completing a course?

Once you complete a course at Innovative Academy, we provide you with:
  • Certification exam preparation: Practice exams, study guides, and mock tests to help you get exam-ready.
  • Additional Resources: Access to study materials and the latest updates on exam formats.
  • Trainer Support: Your trainers will provide guidance and address any questions you may have related to certification exams.
