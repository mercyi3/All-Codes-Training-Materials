AWS Certifications & Exams

What are the different types of AWS certifications available?

  • SysOps Administrator
  • AWS Certification Tracks
  • Foundational Certifications
  • Associate Certifications
  • Professional Certifications
  • Specialty Certifications

Certification through AWS is valid for three years from the date it was earned. Before the three-year period expires, you must recertify to keep your certification current and active.

What is the passing score for AWS certification exams?

The examination is scored against a minimum standard established by AWS professionals who are guided by certification industry best practices and guidelines. Your results for the examination are reported as a score from 100-1,000, with a minimum passing score of 720.

How can I schedule an AWS certification exam?

  • Click and enter your AWS Certification account.
  • Select “Manage PSI Exams”.
  • Choose the exam you want to take and click “Schedule”.
  • Select the “Remote Online Proctored Exam” delivery mode.
  • Select your preferred Exam Language and Proctor Language (Mandarin or English).
  • Choose the location and time zone.
  • Complete your payment.

Can I take AWS certification exams online or do I have to go to a test center?

You will use the testing center computer to take your exam, and on-site staff will assist you with checking in and storing your belongings. Appointment availability varies by testing center. To find a testing center near you, search availability by ZIP Code.
