Why DevOps ?

IT Roles are moving Significantly towards Cloud-Focused Responsibilities as Platforms provide more Flexible, Scalable, and Efficient Services that can support Numerous Use Cases and Applications across Organizations in their Everyday Functioning. Thus, Learning DevOps is Becoming a Necessity rather than a Requirement for Shaping One's Career.

  • Cloud Focus: Cloud Integration
  • Faster Delivery: Speed Optimization
  • Improved Collaboration: Team Synergy
  • Efficiency: Process Automation
  • Competitive Advantage: Market Leadership

DevOps Job Market Survey in India & Abroad

As per the survey of 2023, India is a growing hub for IT services and product development, with Average Salary of ₹6,00,000 and ₹15,00,000 per year, varying by skill set and experience..

The USA has one of the highest demands for DevOps engineers due to its robust technology sector and focus on cloud adoption and CI/CD practices. With Average Annual Salary of around $120,000 to $150,000 depending on experience and location.

The UK’s demand for DevOps engineers is driven by financial institutions, e-commerce, and government digitization projects. Earns between £50,000 and £80,000 annually

In Australiait is rising as businesses shift to cloud platforms like AWS and Azure. With Annual salaries range from AUD 110,000 to AUD 150,000, with Sydney and Melbourne leading in hiring activity
